  • Handmade Lighter Cases

Materials

All the cases are made for demensions of Criket lighters. Main materials are brass and bronze. All parts brazed with tin solder

Drawing

Every Lighter Case has its unique drawing which could be changed according to customer wishes

Technology

The volumetric pattern is achieved by etching a brass or bronze plate in a solution of ferric chloride

Method

The main method of applying the pattern to prepare for etching is the transfer of toner to the metal surface by heating

Assembly process

After etching, the metal plate is bent in the shape of the case, and the excess metal is tapped with a file

The soldering process

At the end of the assembly process, the molded case is soldered to the side decorative plate and bottom

Bring to a shine

After soldering the main parts, cleaning, grinding and polishing of the case surface is performed

Last stage

Next, the surface of the case is covered with a patina in ammonia vapor, and polished again to give a glossy surface
